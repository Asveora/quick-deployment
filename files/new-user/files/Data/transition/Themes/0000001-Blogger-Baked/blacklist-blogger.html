Blacklist
This is a sample blacklist that allows users to see what a blacklist is and how it functions. Blacklists allow users to readily keep track of those who they have blocked across protocols or set to Do-Not-Interact (DNI) within their circles. Blacklists can be employed by individual users, communities, wheelcores, landhubs, systems, federations, and any other kind of entity within Asveora. Some blacklists may be publicly shared for others to reference such as Asveora's network blacklist or kept private depending on the needs of the blacklist's author.

What you are reading right now is the blacklist's description where you can detail how the blacklist functions and its use cases. To make a blacklist private simply remove it from your library index, store it on a .html page that is not named 'blacklist' to avoid others being able to view it, and do not share a link to it. Blacklists are not just limited to entities within Asveora either. You can also add websites, organizations, or individuals and groups from other platforms to your blacklist as well.
