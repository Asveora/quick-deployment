Forum Rooms
Welcome to the forum rooms! This section allows you feature various rooms, channels, and spaces where different conversations are being held. The default forum table is split into three columns. The first column lists a link to the room, channel, or space where the conversation is being held. The second column lists a link to the conversation itself within the room, channel, or space. The third column lists a link to the protocol client or platform used to access the conversation. Remember to follow Asveora's guidelines when participating in the community.
